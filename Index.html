About Brian Caper

Brian Caper is a skilled economic development professional with nearly 15 years of experience working in city economic growth, urban planning, and workforce development. He has a strong background in finance and urban and regional planning and holds an Executive MBA from the University of Florida.

Throughout his career, Brian Caper has worked on large redevelopment projects, public-private partnerships, and economic programs designed to support local communities. He has served in senior leadership positions, including Economic & Workforce Development Director and Managing Director for City Development, where he managed multimillion-dollar budgets, major infrastructure assets, and large redevelopment efforts.

My Mission

To drive inclusive and sustainable economic growth by leading strategic urban development, strengthening workforce systems, and fostering strong public-private partnerships that create long-term community value.
